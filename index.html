Most Visited Locations

From historical monuments, to modern architectual buildings, to caves and more... Here are the most visited places in Kyiv

Pechersk Lavra

Also known as the Kyiv Monastery of the Caves, is a historic Eastern Orthodox Christian monastery which gave its name to one of the city districts where it is located.

The Motherland Monument

Monumental statue that is a part of the National Museum of the History of Ukraine in the Second World War.

St Andrew's Church

Orthodox church constructed between 1747 and 1754, a rare example of Elizabethan Baroque in Ukraine. Situated on a steep, the church overlooks the historic Podil neighborhood.
