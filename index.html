Most Visited Cities

From historical monuments, to modern architectual buildings, to caves and more... Here are the most visited cities in Ukraine

Kyiv

Kiev is the incredible capital of Ukraine. With medieval monuments and mighty Soviet-style architecture, it's the grand getaway to Eastern Europe.

Odessa

Odessa is the third-largest city in Ukraine and a major port on the mythical Black Sea. Its layout gives it an intimate feel, with narrow winding roads and plenty of secluded squares.

Lviv

Lviv is one of the most important cultural centres in Ukraine. Travellers gravitate here to visit the city's UNESCO-protected Old Town. It's the largest city in western Ukraine.

Blog & News

Welcome to the Blog & News block, here you will find a wide variety of articles and useful information for your trips.

Maidan Nezalezhnosti

This Independence Square is the central square of Kyiv, the capital city of Ukraine. One of the city's main squares, it is located on Khreshchatyk Street in the Shevchenko Raion. The square has been knownunder many different names, but often it is called simply Maidan ("square").

St.Sophia Cathedral

Architectural monument of Kyivan Rus, it's one of the city's best known landmarks.

Pechersk Lavra

Also known as the Kiev Monastery of the Caves, is a historic Eastern Orthodox Christian monastery.

Motherland

It's a monumental statue in Kyiv and it's a part of the National Museum of the History of Ukraine in the Second World War.
