Most Visited Locations in Kyiv

From historical monuments, to modern architectual buildings, to caves and more... Here are the most visited places in Kyiv

Pechersk Lavra

Known as the Monastery of the Caves, is a historic Eastern Orthodox Christian monastery which gave its name to one of the city districts.

The Motherland Monument

Monumental statue of a woman that is a part of the National Museum of the History of Ukraine in the Second World War.

St Andrew's Church

Orthodox church situated on a steep constructed between 1747 and 1754, a rare example of Elizabethan Baroque in Ukraine.

Maidan Nezalezhnosti

This Independence Square is the central square of Kyiv, the capital city of Ukraine. One of the city's main squares, it is located on Khreshchatyk Street in the Shevchenko Raion. The square has been knownunder many different names, but often it is called simply Maidan ("square").

St.Sophia Cathedral

Architectural monument of Kyivan Rus, it's one of the city's best known landmarks.

Golden Gate

It was the main gate in the 11th century fortifications of Kyiv.

Comfort Town

It's the product of the Archimatika architectural firm.
